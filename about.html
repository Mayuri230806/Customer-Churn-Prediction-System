About Our Project

Customer Churn Prediction is a Machine Learning application that helps businesses identify customers who are likely to leave their services. By analyzing customer information such as contract type, monthly charges, tenure, internet services, and payment methods, the system predicts churn in advance. This enables companies to improve customer satisfaction, reduce revenue loss, and build long-term customer relationships.

Machine Learning

Our system uses Logistic Regression to analyze customer behavior and accurately predict whether a customer is likely to churn.

Business Insights

Organizations can identify high-risk customers early and offer personalized discounts, better plans, or support to improve customer retention.

Reliable Predictions

The model is trained using customer data after preprocessing, encoding, and feature engineering to provide reliable and consistent predictions.
